## Full description (4000 characters max)

Hisaab is a simple, no-fuss way to track two things: money people owe you (or you owe them), and your everyday home expenses.

**Khata — track balances with people**
Keep a running ledger for anyone you lend to, borrow from, or do business with. Record what you gave and what you got back, and Hisaab keeps a running balance automatically — so you always know who owes what.

**Home Expenses**
Log your day-to-day spending by category — groceries, rent, utilities, and more. See monthly totals, category breakdowns, and a full year view to spot spending trends.

**Built for how you actually use it**
• Works entirely offline — no internet or account required
• Multiple ledgers — separate your shop's khata from personal accounts
• Attach photos of receipts or bills to any entry
• Voice input for quick hands-free entries
• Set monthly budgets per category with visual warnings
• Search, filter, and edit any past entry
• Export your data anytime as a backup file or CSV (Excel-compatible)
• Optional PIN lock for privacy
• Light and dark mode
• Supports 13 currencies

**Your data stays yours**
Hisaab stores everything on your device — nothing is uploaded anywhere unless you choose to export it yourself. No ads, no tracking, no hidden fees.

Whether you're a shop owner keeping customer accounts, or just want a clear picture of your household spending, Hisaab keeps it simple.
