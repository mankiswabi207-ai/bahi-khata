Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 14, 2026

Hisaab ("the app") is a personal ledger and home-expense tracker. This policy explains what data the app handles and how.

What data the app stores

Hisaab stores the following information, entirely on your own device, inside your browser's local storage:

  • Names of people/parties in your khata, and the entries you record for them (amounts, notes, dates)
  • Home expense entries (amounts, categories, notes, dates)
  • Photos you choose to attach to entries (stored as compressed images on-device)
  • App preferences: currency, theme, budgets, categories, and an optional PIN you set yourself

Where your data lives

All of the data above is stored locally in your device's browser storage. It is not transmitted to, or stored on, any server operated by us, unless you explicitly enable a cloud sync feature in the future (if and when that feature is added, this policy will be updated to describe it before it is turned on).

Data we do not collect

We do not collect, receive, or have access to your khata entries, expense records, photos, or any personal information you enter into the app. We do not run analytics or advertising trackers inside the app.

Your control over your data

  • You can export a full backup of your data at any time from Settings → Data → Export backup.
  • You can delete any entry, party, or ledger at any time within the app.
  • Clearing your browser's site data, or uninstalling the app, permanently deletes all locally stored data — export a backup first if you want to keep it.

Permissions the app may request

  • Camera / Photo library — only used if you choose to attach a photo to an entry. Never accessed without your action.
  • Microphone — only used if you tap the voice-input (mic) button to dictate an entry. Never accessed without your action.

Children's privacy

Hisaab is a general-purpose finance utility and is not directed at children. We do not knowingly collect data from children, and since the app stores data only on-device, no data is collected by us from any user.

Changes to this policy

If this app adds features that change how data is handled (for example, an optional cloud sync), this page will be updated first, with the date above revised accordingly.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to the developer contact listed on the app's Play Store page.
